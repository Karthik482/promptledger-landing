Common Questions

Things finance teams actually ask.

Does my data ever leave my warehouse?
In v0.1, uploaded CSVs are loaded into an in-memory DuckDB instance scoped to your browser session — destroyed when you close the tab, nothing persisted server-side. Coming soon: direct warehouse connectors where SQL runs in your Snowflake or BigQuery instance, under your IAM. Query results are rendered and discarded — never stored, never trained on.
How is this different from ChatGPT with a SQL plugin?
ChatGPT guesses your schema and hallucinates metric definitions. PromptLedger uses authored skills with audited SQL templates and a semantic layer. Every answer is reproducible, governed, and citable.
Who writes the skills?
Start with the included library — finance skills covering ARR, NRR, churn, runway, headcount, and cohort analysis. Customize them for your schema, or author new ones in YAML + Markdown. Most skills can be authored in under an hour.
